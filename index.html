Who We Are

GBS Trade is a global commodities trading company with over a decade of experience in the acquisition, commercialization, and distribution of raw materials across metals, energy, agricultural, and soft commodities.

We don't just trade commodities — we build long-term relationships that drive sustainable growth and operational success for our clients worldwide, operating across strategic markets in Latin America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East.
